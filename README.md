AI-powered post trimming tool designed to help you stay within X (Twitter) character limits while maintaining your message's impact.

## Overview

TrimTweet uses advanced AI to intelligently shorten your social media posts, ensuring they fit perfectly within platform constraints without losing your voice or key message.

### Current Focus

**X (Twitter) - Free Tier Optimization**

The first iteration focuses exclusively on trimming posts for X's 280-character limit, targeting the free tier user experience. Our AI preserves your tone, adds relevant hashtags, and ensures your trimmed content stays engaging.

### Roadmap

- ✅ X (Twitter) character limit optimization
- 🔜 LinkedIn post formatting (coming soon)
- 🔜 Threads support (coming soon)
- 🔜 Multi-platform trimming (future iterations)

## Features

- **Smart Trimming:** AI-powered content reduction that maintains meaning
- **Voice Preservation:** Keeps your unique writing style intact
- **Auto Hashtags:** Generates 3-5 relevant hashtags automatically
- **Real-time Character Count:** Live feedback as you type
- **Professional UI:** Modern, glassmorphic design with smooth animations

## Technology

Built with React, powered by Google's Gemini AI, and designed for a seamless user experience.
